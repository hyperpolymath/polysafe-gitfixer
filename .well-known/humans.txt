/* PHILOSOPHY */

Each component uses the language with the strongest safety guarantees
for its specific concerns:
- Rust for memory safety and RAII
- Haskell for totality and streaming
- Elixir for fault tolerance and supervision
- Idris for dependent types and proofs
- Nickel for configuration schemas
